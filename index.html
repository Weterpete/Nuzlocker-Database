So... What is a "Nuzlocke"?

A nuzlocke is a set of self-imposed rules one can put on a playthrough of a Pokemon game, it was invented by Nick Franco's Pokemon Hard-Mode comic (found here), and is generally the most popular challenge to play through a Pokemon game with. The main rule is permadeath, that if a pokemon faints, it is dead forever, you can learn more about that in the rulesets section of this very website. You may not think it, but Pokemon, "the baby game", can actually be a made into a very thought provoking experience when nuzlocking, which is why I love the challenge so much that I made a website.

And if this is all going straight over your head, you need not worry, because that's what this website is for: readying you to step into the next Pokemon journey of the rest of your life.
